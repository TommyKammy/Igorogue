# v0.2.7 Design Patch — Temporary Liberty Expiry

## Added

- ADR-0014: enemy-turn-end temporary-liberty expiry sweep
- FEAT-011: effect anchor、stack、simultaneous expiry / capture
- FEAT-005 accepted capture and sacrifice trigger order
- TLE-01〜TLE-15 specification fixtures and checker
- reserved qi and deferred player choice domain contracts

## Changed

- temporary liberties remain active through both normal and counterattack enemy actions
- all due effects expire before a single global capture snapshot
- simultaneous black and white king capture is a player loss
- terminal capture batches suppress all benefits
- expiry capture occurs before enemy-turn-end counterattack gain
- mandatory expiry capture may revisit a historical topology
- Spirit Spring is a continuous modifier rather than a timed effect

## Evidence level

E1 specification and deterministic fixture checker. Product Rules Kernel and human playtest remain unimplemented.
